Privacy Policy for RecipEat

Last Updated: October 15, 2025
Effective Date: October 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to RecipEat, a mobile application developed and operated by Hyperdrive Machine (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the RecipEat mobile application (the “App”). By using RecipEat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hyperdrivemachine@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Password (securely encrypted)

User-Generated Content

  • Recipes, photos, and instructions
  • Meal plans and nutrition logs
  • Shopping lists and home inventory
  • Meal templates and reminders

Device Information

  • Device ID and app preferences

Subscription Data

  • Subscription status, product ID, purchase dates, and store information

Photos and Camera Access

With your permission, the app may access your camera or photo library to allow you to capture or upload recipe images. These may be processed using AI to extract recipe information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the RecipEat service
  • Manage your account and preferences
  • Store and sync recipes, photos, and meal data
  • Process photos for recipe extraction
  • Send notifications or respond to inquiries

Note: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.

3. Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Cloud: Supabase (secure cloud database)
  • Local: Preferences stored securely on your device

Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security updates

We encourage you to use strong passwords and report any security concerns to hyperdrivemachine@gmail.com.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat – Privacy Policy
  • Apple – Privacy Policy
  • Google – Privacy Policy

No analytics, ad networks, or data brokers are used.

5. Subscription and Payment Information

Payments are processed by your app store (Google Play or Apple) via RevenueCat. We do not access or store your credit card information.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is shared only with Supabase and RevenueCat to provide app functionality. We may disclose data only if required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, update, export, or delete your data
  • Manage email and push notification preferences
  • Request account deletion via hyperdrivemachine@gmail.com

8. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account remains active. Deleted accounts are fully removed within 30 days, with backups retained for up to 90 days for security recovery.

9. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users and Data Transfers

Your data may be stored in the U.S. or EU. EU users have GDPR rights including access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have rights to know, delete, and opt-out of data sale (we do not sell data). Email hyperdrivemachine@gmail.com with the subject “California Privacy Rights Request.”

12. Do Not Track

RecipEat does not track users across other websites or apps.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last Updated” date will reflect the most recent version, and major changes will be communicated via email or in-app notice.
